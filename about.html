I am Isabel Guitteau I am a fourth year Environmental Systems Ecology and Evolution major at UCSD This summer I was part of an 8 week program that took place in four UC reserves, My main project took place at Landels Hill Big Creek Rerserve. There my project looked at Herbivory at the intersection of shrub and grasslands, We utilized granivory surveys, herbivory exclusions, and vegetation surveys. We used JMP Statistical software and excell to analyze our data. This year I joined an internship for my senior project on California Poppys at Dr.Clelands lab at UCSD Biological Sciences department. There I am part of a study on assisted gene flow as a way to mitigate climate change caused drought in California. I am also in the midst of pursuing a studio art minor and hope to interconnect Ecology and Art in the future!
